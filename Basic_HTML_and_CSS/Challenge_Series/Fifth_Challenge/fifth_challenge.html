The Penguin Conservatory

Welcome to the Penguin Conservatory

We at the Penguin Conservatory work to reduce the threats to endangered penguins worldwide.

Some species, such as the Galapagos Penguin, have populations as low as 1,200.

A common threat is climate change, which can greatly impact habitat and food availability.

Our orginization works to both reduce climate change factors while directly caring for the most endangered species by assisting in habitat conservation.
